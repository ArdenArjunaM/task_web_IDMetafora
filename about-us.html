Our Journey

IDMETAFORA Indonesia Teknologi is an Information Technology Solutions specialist based in Central Java, Indonesia. Our technologies and systems are used to manage and integrate core business processes. Our Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software offers single system solutions that integrate processes across the business.

IDMETAFORA was founded as an ERP development company with an expert team. Since we started the business, we have been providing software development services to clients from Java, Sumatra, Borneo, Sulawesi, and other countries in Asia, Australia, and Europe. We are one of the leading web development companies in Indonesia.

With dedication and passion for ERP development, we have experienced rapid growth and remain dynamic within the digital industry. We are committed to consistently delivering the best results, services, and offerings to each IDMETAFORA client.
